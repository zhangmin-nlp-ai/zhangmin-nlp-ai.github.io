Publications

As of 18 March, 2026, Min Zhang has published over 300 papers in top-tier AI journals and conferences (24,000+ Google Scholar citations). He has received five best paper awards at top international conferences (ACM Multimedia 2022, COLING 2022, NLPCC 2020, CCKS 2020, CCKS 2019), authored two Springer monographs in English and one chapter in the Routledge Encyclopedia of Translation Technology, and edited 16 English-language proceedings published by Springer, IEEE CPS, and others.

According to CSRankings, he is No. 1 worldwide in both team and individual categories in AI Fundamentals and Natural Language Processing over the past five years (2021-2025). For a detailed list of publications, please visit: https://dblp.org/pid/83/5342-5.html.
