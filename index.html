Min Zhang（张民）
PhD, Distinguished Professor
Distinguished Assistant Principal, Dean of the Institute of Computing and Intelligence, Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), China
Dean, School of Future Science and Engineering, Soochow University, China

  • ACL Fellow (For significant contributions to MT, Parsing and sustained service to the growth of NLP in China and Southeast Asia)
  • AAIA Fellow (For significant contributions to AI, with special emphasis on his sustained contributions to the development of AI in Asia)
  • 5 Best Paper awards at top conference (ACM MM 2022, COLING 2022, etc)
  • No. 1 worldwide in CSRankings (AI Fundamentals & NLP, 2022-2026) in both team and individual.
  • 6 science and technology awards at provincial/ministerial level and from national first-tier academic societies.
  • Chief Scientist of the NSF Innovative Research Group Project (Category A): Project “Natural Language-Centric New-Generation Artificial Intelligence”
  • Winner of the China National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholar (Project: “Structural Analysis of Natural Language and Machine Translation”)
  • The National Hundred, Thousand and Ten Thousand Talent, the Middle-aged and Young experts with Outstanding Contributions to the Nation, and the State Council Special Allowance Expert
  • Led the development of Lychee Uni-MoE, a language-centric Omni-MoE large model with complete IP and full-stack technological autonomy and controllability—the first and only such model from a domestic university to receive dual certification from both MIIT and CAC of China.
